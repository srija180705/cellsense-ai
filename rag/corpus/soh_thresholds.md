# Battery State-of-Health Thresholds and Actions

## Healthy band
A battery with State of Health (SoH) at or above 85% is considered healthy. Continue normal operation and perform routine inspection at each scheduled maintenance interval. No load restrictions are required.

## Watch band
SoH between 78% and 85% indicates measurable degradation. Increase monitoring frequency, review the asset's duty cycle for excessive load, and begin replacement planning and budgeting so procurement lead time does not become a constraint.

## Critical band
SoH below 78% indicates advanced degradation approaching end of life. Prioritise the asset for replacement scheduling, verify its thermal behaviour, and avoid assigning it to high-load or long-range duties until replaced.

## End of life
End of life is commonly defined between 70% and 80% of rated capacity, where usable range, reliability, and safety margins fall below operational requirements. Replacement should be completed before this threshold is reached.
